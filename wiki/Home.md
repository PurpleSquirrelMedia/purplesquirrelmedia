# Purple Squirrel Media Wiki

Welcome to the official Purple Squirrel Media documentation wiki!

## About Purple Squirrel Media

Purple Squirrel Media is a blockchain development organization deeply embedded in the **Solana ecosystem**. We build tools, applications, and solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible on Solana and beyond.

> Just like the mythical 'purple squirrel' in recruiting, we aim to be the perfect match for the evolving needs of the Solana network and the broader digital economy.

## Our Focus Areas

- **NFT Infrastructure** - Candy Machine implementations, Metaplex integrations, and marketplace solutions
- **Token Development** - PURP token and tokenomics implementation
- **DeFi Applications** - Lending protocols, trading bots, and financial tools
- **AI + Blockchain** - Customer review AI systems, stock prediction bots
- **Cross-Chain Solutions** - Wormhole integrations and cross-chain tooling
